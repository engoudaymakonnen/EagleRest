Eagles Rest Team





“Knowing your true identity brings focus and rest”




Jan Limero



Jan Limiero is the co-founder of Eagles Rest, a ministry which provides encouragement and equipping to kingdom-minded leaders, where she serves as a Life Coach and Spiritual Director.

A Kingsport native, Jan served alongside her husband in local church ministries for more than two decades. She holds a Bachelor of Arts from East Tennessee State University, is a faculty member of the Leadership Institute where she earned a certificate in spiritual formation and leadership and spiritual direction, and is a member of the Evangelical Spiritual Directors Association. Jan is on Stadia’s Bloom leadership team where she leads a team who cares for women in church planting. She has developed and taught seminars and classes in forgiveness, identity, prayer and spiritual warfare. Her passion is coming alongside ministry leaders to help them discover their identity and calling, which leads to greater focus and rest


“Is the life you’re living now the life you want to pass on to your disciples? Or your children?”




David Limero



After decades of successful ministry, that question from the Lord started David’s personal journey to learn how to live and lead from overflow instead of overwhelm.David Limiero photo David serves as the Senior Director of Strategic Services at Stadia, a global church-planting mission, where he leverages platforms and processes to free up people for ministry.

He fell in love with east Tennessee and his future wife Jan when he came here for seminary in 1989. David served churches in Colorado, North Carolina, Tennessee, Illinois, and California before returning to Kingsport in 2017. He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Colorado and Master of Divinity from Emmanuel Christian Seminary. https://linkedin.com/davidlimiero









Board of Directors





Tom Wymore



Tom, has been pastoring, training, and coaching God’s children for almost fifty years. His passion and life mission is to live in increasing intimacy with God and influence as many others as possible to do the same. A husband, father, and grandfather, Tom lives in Texas with his wife Charlie.


Chris and Jaime



Chris and Jaime Hantla are identity coaches who equip in the areas of identity, listening prayer and inner-healing. They have a passion for seeing spiritual orphans wake up to the reality of their adoption in Christ Jesus as sons and daughters of our heavenly Father and then take hold of their inheritance as kings and queens in His royal courts. Each earned the Master’s of Arts in Cross-Cultural Studies from Fuller Seminary.
